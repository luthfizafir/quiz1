Kue Apem

Different from general kue apem which use rice flour
and coconut sugar, there is something special about
this kue apem. The addition of cassava to the dough
makes the cake taste more delicious, sweet and has
a distinctive aroma. For those who like drinking
coffee in the afternoon, this typical South Tangerang
food is suitable as an accompanying snack

Estimated price : Rp2.000,00/cake
